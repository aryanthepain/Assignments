# CULTURAL BOARD CONSTITUTION

## Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati

# PREAMBLE

At the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, **we, the students**, stand as a testament to intellectual prowess, having navigated fierce competition to secure our place. Yet, our aspirations extend far beyond academic excellence; we arrive with an innate drive to learn, to thrive, and to cultivate the profound connection between scientific ingenuity and cultural richness. Indeed, history shows that the most brilliant minds are often the most culturally attuned.

With a commitment to enriching the cultural fabric of the Institute, fostering creative expression, and nurturing artistic talent, **we, the Cultural Board**, stand as the cornerstone of cultural life at IIT Guwahati. We recognise this as a pivotal time to shape our collective cultural journey, celebrating the diverse traditions and vibrant creativity that define our community.

From spirited inter-hostel competitions to grand annual festivals, students aspire to showcase their talents and create unforgettable experiences. The Cultural Board is poised to empower these aspirations, providing not only a platform for expression but also steadfast support to realize their ambitions, leaving an indelible mark on the landscape of arts and culture.

Drawing from the foundational ethos of the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, **we, the members of the Cultural Board**, pledge to uphold the following principles:

- To address and resolve cultural and artistic concerns within our community.
- To advocate for the interests of all students in matters pertaining to arts and culture.
- To ensure equitable access to cultural resources, venues, and opportunities for all clubs and individuals.
- To uphold the principles of artistic excellence and creative integrity in all our endeavors.
- To celebrate and embrace the diversity of different artistic disciplines.
- To provide platforms for the cultivation of artistic skills and talent.
- To foster a culture of collaboration and cooperation in all artistic and cultural endeavors within our campus community.

In pursuit of these objectives, we commit to honoring and abiding by this Constitution, demonstrating maturity in our approach, and cultivating an atmosphere characterized by mutual respect, trust, and synergy in all our artistic and cultural endeavors.

## I. The Cultural Board

The organisation established under this Constitution shall be known as the Cultural Board of IIT Guwahati.
The authority of this Constitution shall serve as the foundational basis for all governance, decision-making, and administrative functions of the Cultural Board.

## II. Membership

All registered students of the Institute who actively participate in Cultural Clubs, hold positions as club members or event managers, or serve as Council members within the Cultural Board shall be considered members of the Cultural Community of IIT Guwahati.
All such members shall adhere to the provisions of this Constitution and the regulations established by the Cultural Board.

## III. Power

All authority vested in bodies authorized under this Constitution, as well as in the by-laws and regulations of the Cultural Board, derives its legitimacy from the Cultural Community.
The Cultural Board shall govern, represent, and remain directly accountable to the Cultural Community.

## IV. Precedence

This Constitution shall take precedence over all other instruments governing the Cultural Board or any of its subordinate bodies.
All Cultural Clubs, Events, Fests, and associated Cultural Board entities shall operate in accordance with the provisions of this Constitution.
In the event of any conflict between this Constitution and subordinate governing documents, this Constitution shall prevail, subject to Institute regulations.

## V. Branches of the Cultural Board

The Cultural Board shall be divided into the following branches:

- Cultural Board Council
- Cultural Clubs of IIT Guwahati
- Cultural Fests of IIT Guwahati
- Events conducted under the Cultural Board
- The magazine Roz-Roz

### A. Cultural Board Council

The Cultural Board Secretary, the Overall Coordinator, and the Domain Heads shall constitute the Cultural Board Council.

### B. Cultural Clubs of IIT Guwahati

Each Cultural Club shall consist of:

- Club Secretary
- Overall Coordinator
- Domain Heads
- Inter-IIT Cup Mentor

These office bearers shall collectively govern the operations of the Cultural Club.

### C. Events under Cultural Board

Each Cultural Board Event shall be managed by:

- Event Convenor
- Overall Coordinator
- Module Heads

### D. Fests under Cultural Board

Each Cultural Fest shall be governed by:

- Fest Convenor
- Overall Coordinator
- Module Heads

### E. Roz-Roz

The magazine Roz-Roz shall be managed by:

- Editor-in-Chief
- Content Head and Content Team
- Design Head and Design Team

## VI. Exercise of Powers

No person belonging to one branch of the Cultural Board shall exercise powers pertaining to any other branch unless expressly authorized by this Constitution.
All powers shall be exercised strictly in accordance with the authority granted under this Constitution.

## VII. Positions of Responsibility (PoR)

Any student member of a Cultural Board body who possesses executive, administrative, or decision-making authority shall be considered to hold a Position of Responsibility (PoR).
All Positions of Responsibility shall operate under the authority and provisions of this Constitution.
